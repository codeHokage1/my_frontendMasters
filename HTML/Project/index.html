About the Uchiha Clan

By: @itachi_12 | 12th May, 1987

Uchiha clan is one of the most powerful clans in the history of Konoha. They are known for their sharingan, a special eye that allows them to see the future and copy jutsu. They are also known for their hatred of the Hyuga clan, who are known for their Byakugan, a special eye that allows them to see the past.

Is Minato the fastest Shinobi?

By: @raikage | 12th May, 2000

Minato is one of the fastest shinobi in the history of Konoha. He is known for his speed and his ability to teleport. He is also known for his son, Naruto, who is the fastest shinobi in the history of Konoha. So technically, Naruto is the fastets Shinobi.
